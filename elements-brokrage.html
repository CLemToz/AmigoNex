Virtual Efficiency in Every Deal

AmigoNex partnered with Element Real Estate Brokers, a dynamic brokerage team focused on modern home transactions. They approached us to simplify their transaction coordination and bring consistency to their property listing process, which was slowing down their deal closures.

Our goal was to minimize operational stress so their agents could focus more on relationships and results.

With our Virtual Assistant service, we implemented a remote transaction coordination system using Flex MLS, ensuring timely listing updates, proper document handling, and smooth communication throughout each deal.

For a growing brokerage like Element, where every hour matters, the time saved per transaction created immediate value. Our seamless backend support allowed their team to move faster and close with confidence—every time.

Challenge & Solution

Element was growing fast but lacked an organized system for managing listings and transaction flow. Deadlines were being missed, and communication gaps were common.
AmigoNex introduced a dedicated VA model that handled property listing entry, coordinated with buyers and sellers, tracked milestones, and ensured compliance — all through Flex MLS and daily check-ins.

Final Result

Closings became faster. Clients got updates on time. Agents finally had room to focus on what they do best: selling homes. Element saw a marked improvement in client satisfaction and internal workflow clarity within just a few weeks.
